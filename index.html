Raghad's Story

I starting to draw when I was a little child. I still remember spending every second of my childhood times drawing and painting. A few years growing up, I tried drawing with a pencil I used to draw houses, trees, and parts of the face. When I joined high school all my paintings where destroyed And I couldn’t continue drawing and painting. After 3 years, in 2017 my journey with charcoal began. I took a course on how to draw using charcoal and boom! Here I am. Since then I never stopped drawing and I will never quit for any reason. It’s a life for me.
